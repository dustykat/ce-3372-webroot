CE 3372 Water Systems Design

Lesson 7. Pumping Systems

Water moves from higher to lower energy - Path of least resistance - Head is energy per unit weight of a fluid - Pumps are used to add energy to move water to a higher elevation, or over a barrier, or to increase system pressure.
